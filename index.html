Biography

Welcome! I am a master’s student of Artificial Intelligence and a junior research assistant in the Natural Language Processing lab at the school of ECE at the University of Tehran. I completed my bachelor in Computer Engineering at the Ferdowsi University of Mashhad.

My research focuses on creating parallel corpus for neural machine translation systems via unsupervised sentence mining methods. I’ve been fortunate to have Dr. Hesham Feili and Dr. Mohammad Javad Dousti as my supervisors on this topics.
